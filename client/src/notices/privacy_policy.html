MODBOX Privacy Policy

1. Welcome

Welcome to MODBOX. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains the information we collect, how we use it, and how we protect it. By using MODBOX, you agree to the practices described here.

2. Information We Collect

  • Account information: username, email, and age
  • User-generated content: any content you create, upload, or share within the app
  • Device information: device type, operating system, and app version
  • Usage data / Analytics: information on how you interact with MODBOX, feature usage, and error reports

Note: MODBOX does not use cookies. All data is collected directly through the app.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Operate, maintain, and improve MODBOX
  • Analyze app usage and feature popularity through analytics
  • Communicate updates, changes, and support messages
  • Monitor and maintain safety, preventing misuse or inappropriate content

4. How We Share Your Information

  • Internal use only: accessed by MODBOX staff to maintain and improve the app
  • Third-party services: shared only with analytics or crash-reporting tools
  • Legal requirements: may be disclosed if required by law or to protect MODBOX rights

We do not sell or trade your personal information.

5. Security

We implement reasonable measures to protect your information, including account data and user content. While we strive to maintain security, no system is completely secure. Users are responsible for safeguarding their account credentials.

6. Your Choices

  • Update or delete your account information at any time
  • Opt out of optional data sharing if available
  • Contact us for any questions or concerns regarding your information

7. Children’s Privacy

MODBOX is intended for users 13 years and older. Users under 13 must have parental or guardian permission to use the app. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

8. Updates to Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. The latest version will always be available within the app. Please check periodically to stay informed about how we collect and use your information.

9. Contact / Questions
